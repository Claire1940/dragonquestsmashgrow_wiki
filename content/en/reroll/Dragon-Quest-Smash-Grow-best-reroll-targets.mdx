## Optimizing Your Settings for Combat

Once you have secured your **Dragon Quest Smash Grow best reroll targets**, you need to ensure the game runs smoothly. The visual effects in 2026 can become overwhelming, making it difficult to see incoming projectiles.

1.  **Performance Mode:** Navigate to Settings > System and toggle "Performance Mode" to ON. This makes certain environmental effects semi-transparent, allowing you to see your character clearly during chaotic battles.
2.  **60 FPS Toggle:** Ensure your frame rate is set to 60 FPS for responsive dodging.
3.  **Ultimate Animations:** Turn off the "Coup de Grace" (Ultimate) animations. While beautiful, they pause the gameplay and can break your rhythm during intense farming sessions.

## Advanced Systems: Memories and the AFK Search

Beyond weapons, your power in Dragon Quest Smash Grow comes from "Memories"—small creatures you equip to your characters. These provide passive stat boosts and can be arranged in specific orders to trigger set bonuses. You can learn more about the deep lore and mechanics of these systems on the [official Dragon Quest website](https://dragonquest.square-enix-games.com/en-us/) or community hubs.

### The AFK Search System
To keep up with the resource demands of the Forge, you must utilize the "Search" area. This is an AFK (Away From Keyboard) mechanic where you send robots on 12-hour journeys.
*   **Priority:** Always keep your robots deployed.
*   **Rewards:** You will receive gold, upgrade materials, and occasionally rare Memories.
*   **Upgrading:** Use your early-game resources to upgrade the robots themselves to increase the quality of loot they return.
