## Optimizing Your Settings for Combat

Dragon Quest Smash Grow can be visually intensive, especially when the screen is filled with projectiles. To ensure you aren't losing frames during a crucial boss fight, you should adjust your system settings immediately after finishing your rerolls.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Frame Rate** | **60 FPS** | Essential for dodging telegraphs and smooth movement. |
| **Performance Mode** | **ON** | Makes some effects semi-transparent so you can see your character. |
| **Ultimate Animation** | **OFF** | Stops the gameplay from pausing every time you use a Coup de Grace. |
| **Graphics Quality** | **High/Ultra** | Only if your device supports it; otherwise, prioritize FPS. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Performance Mode is highly recommended for higher-level gauntlets. Without it, the "bullet hell" nature of the game can make it impossible to see incoming attacks through your own skill effects.

## Character Progression and Team Synergy

You start the game as a Swordsman, but you will quickly unlock additional classes. For example, clearing Chapter 2, Stage 2 unlocks the Mage. You can eventually have three characters in your party and swap between them instantly during a run.

*   **Swordsman:** Your primary tank and melee DPS. Use him for clearing small groups or when you need high survivability.
*   **Mage:** Excellent for ranged elemental damage. Switch to the Mage when a boss has a dangerous point-blank AoE attack.
*   **The "Tree" System:** Reaching Adventure Rank 8 unlocks the customization tree. This allows you to invest points into passive abilities and further specialize your classes.

### Using Memories for Stat Boosts
In the Forge area, you can equip "Memories." These are essentially equipment cards that provide bonus stats. Pay close attention to the set bonuses; equipping them in specific orders or matching types can grant massive boosts to your HP and Attack power.

## AFK Rewards and the Search Area
Dragon Quest Smash Grow includes an "idle" mechanic called the Search Area. You can send small robots on "AFK Journeys" that last up to 12 hours. 

1.  **Prioritize Upgrades:** Use your early gold to upgrade your robots. Higher-level robots bring back better loot.
2.  **Resource Gathering:** These journeys are the primary way to get gold and Memory upgrade materials without active grinding.
3.  **Check Regularly:** Even if you aren't playing the story, log in twice a day to collect your rewards and restart the 12-hour timer.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my Dragon Quest Smash Grow reroll weapon later?**

A: You cannot "reset" the infinite reroll once you have accepted the weapon and moved past the tutorial. However, you can pull for more weapons later using premium currency (Gems). This is why it is so important to get a top-tier 3-star weapon like the Platinum Plums for free at the start.

**Q: Does the game work on emulators or tablets?**

A: As of the 2026 launch, many players have reported issues with emulators and certain tablets. The game is currently optimized for mobile phones. If you experience a "stuck on loading" screen, try switching to a mobile device or checking for a compatibility patch.

**Q: How do I unlock the third character slot?**

A: The third character slot is typically unlocked by progressing through the main story mode. Continue clearing chapters and reaching specific Adventure Rank milestones (usually around Rank 5-10) to fill out your full three-person roster.

**Q: What is the benefit of turning off Ultimate Animations?**

A: While the animations look great, they pause the flow of combat. In a fast-paced roguelike, these pauses can be jarring and slow down your farming efficiency. Turning them off allows the "Coup de Grace" to trigger instantly without interrupting the action.
