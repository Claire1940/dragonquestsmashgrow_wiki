To succeed in the later stages of the Giant Quest, you must focus on event-specific character growth. These are special boons or passive abilities that only function within the event world.

For example, some blessings increase your resistance to specific status ailments found in the Tanglemore caves, while others boost your fire damage—a particularly effective element against the slimes and guardsmen encountered early on.

### Recommended Event Blessings

*   **Increased Resistance:** Essential for the dark caves where divine presence is required to see.
*   **Fire Mastery:** Boosts the efficiency of the new fire-based active skills.
*   **Gold Finder:** Increases the drop rate of event coins from standard mobs.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not enter the "Cave to the North" without the light-based blessing. The game will describe it as "void of light," making it impossible to see or target enemies effectively.

## Advanced Dragon Quest Smash Grow Tips for 2026

As you progress through the event, keep these high-level strategies in mind to maximize your efficiency:

1.  **Talk to Everyone:** NPCs like the Castle Guardsmen or the Innkeeper often have multi-stage dialogue. Clicking them multiple times can trigger "Townsfolk Mode" quests that aren't immediately visible on the map.

4.  **Auto-Battle Management:** While the game is an "idol" or "idle" hybrid, some boss encounters (like the giant Slime Boss) require you to take off "Auto" mode to dodge specific heavy attacks, even if your power level is high.
