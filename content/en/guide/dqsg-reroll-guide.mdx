## Top Tier Weapons to Target

Once you have handled your initial pulls, it is time to focus on the infinite reroll banner. Not all three-star weapons are created equal. Some offer raw damage, while others are hampered by restrictive RNG or specific blessing requirements. According to our **dqsg reroll guide** research, the following weapons are the "S-Tier" targets.

### 1. Fire Blade (The Boss Killer)
The **Fire Blade** is widely considered the best weapon in the game for 2026. While its base physical fire damage is 250 (which is lower than the Falcon Blade), its ultimate ability is unparalleled.

*   **Primary Attack:** flaming blade dealing 250% physical fire damage.
*   **Ultimate Ability:** Launches two powerful fire projectiles every time an enemy is defeated.
*   **Utility:** Grants immunity to Freeze for 15 seconds.

The reason the Fire Blade wins is its ultimate. In stages with high enemy density or during boss fights with adds, the projectile generation creates a "snowball effect" of damage that no other weapon can match.

### 2. Falcon Blade (The DPS King)
If you prefer raw, consistent numbers over projectile gimmicks, the **Falcon Blade** is your best bet. It boasts the highest base potency of any melee weapon.

*   **Primary Attack:** Lightning-fast strikes dealing 350% physical wind damage.
*   **Ultimate Ability:** Conjures two wind-infused spheres that orbit the player, increases movement speed, and grants immunity to slows/attack speed debuffs.
*   **Synergy:** Has incredible natural synergy with wind-type blessings, which are among the strongest in the current meta.

## Ranged Options: Shiver Stick vs. Queen's Whip

Not everyone wants to be in the face of a giant dragon. For players who prefer a kiting playstyle, the **dqsg reroll guide** recommends two specific options.

### The Shiver Stick (Control & Safety)
The Shiver Stick is an ice-elemental staff that prioritizes crowd control. It deals 225% magical ice damage, which is higher than the standard 200% found on most other staves. 

> **💡 Tip:** The Shiver Stick's ability to **Freeze** enemies works on almost every opponent in the game. This provides a massive safety net that pure damage weapons lack.

However, its ultimate is a bit counter-intuitive. It triggers an icy counter-attack when you take a hit. As a ranged player, your goal is usually to *avoid* hits, making this ultimate less useful than the Fire Blade's offensive projectiles.

### The Queen’s Whip (Pure Ranged Damage)
The Queen's Whip is a Light-element weapon that focuses on speed. It deals 200% magical light damage and creates a ward that boosts your attack speed for 10 seconds. It is a solid choice if you want to stay at a distance, but it requires "Sword-type Blessings" to maximize its ultimate, which can be subject to RNG.

## Weapons to Avoid During Rerolling

To ensure this **dqsg reroll guide** helps you start with the best possible account, we must highlight the weapons that are currently underperforming. Avoid these in your infinite reroll sessions:

1.  **Platinum Plumes:** These deal dark damage and inflict "Spell Lock." While silence is good in theory, the majority of enemies in Dragon Quest Smash Grow rely on physical attacks or abilities that ignore Spell Lock. The damage is also significantly lower than other options.
2.  **Crystal Claws:** These rely on a "Flinch" mechanic that only triggers on the final hit of a combo. It is too unreliable for high-level play, and the lightning-infused arrow ultimate is mediocre compared to the wind spheres of the Falcon Blade.
3.  **Sandstorm Spear:** This earth-element weapon is locked behind very specific "Spike-type Blessings." If you don't get the right RNG during your run, your ultimate becomes essentially useless.

## Blessing Synergies and Late Game

Choosing your weapon is only half the battle. As you progress through a run in DQSG, you will collect Blessings. Your weapon choice should dictate which Blessings you prioritize.

*   **Wind Blessings:** Pair these with the **Falcon Blade**. The movement speed stacks can make you nearly untouchable.
*   **Arrow/Projectile Blessings:** These have great synergy with the **Shiver Stick**, expanding its area of attack if you collect four or more.
*   **Fire/Attack Power Blessings:** Best for the **Fire Blade** to further enhance the damage of its projectile-on-kill ultimate.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get more than one 3-star weapon from the infinite reroll banner?**

A: No, the infinite reroll banner is designed to give you exactly one guaranteed 3-star weapon of your choice. You cannot "double up" on this specific banner.

**Q: Is the Fire Blade better than the Falcon Blade for beginners?**

A: Yes. While the Falcon Blade has higher base damage, the Fire Blade's ability to clear screens with its ultimate projectiles makes it much more forgiving for new players. This is a key takeaway in any **dqsg reroll guide**.

**Q: Does the element of the weapon matter?**

A: Absolutely. Certain bosses have elemental weaknesses. However, in the early game, the raw power and utility of the Fire Blade or Shiver Stick generally outweigh elemental disadvantages.

**Q: How long does the rerolling process take?**

A: Since the banner is infinite and built-in, it usually takes less than 5 minutes to find the specific weapon you are looking for. There is no need to reinstall the game.
