## Initial Reroll Strategy
Dragon Quest Smash Grow features an infinite reroll system for your starting three-star weapon, which is a fantastic way to ensure you begin the game with top-tier gear. While weapons like the Queen’s Whip or Falcon Blade might appear initially, you should aim for weapons that provide significant utility or damage scaling for your preferred class.

> **💡 Important Note:** The "Platinum Plums" is currently considered one of the strongest weapons available for early-game progression. Do not settle for lesser options during your initial infinite reroll phase.

## Combat Mechanics and Optimization
The gameplay loop revolves around surviving waves of enemies. Because the AI for auto-play is highly efficient at maneuvering and dodging, you should lean into this feature, especially when managing multiple characters. 

To ensure the best performance during intense battles, head to the settings menu. Adjusting your display settings is critical, particularly for rogue-like titles where projectile density can obscure your view.

### Essential Settings for Performance
*   **Frame Rate:** Set to 60 FPS for smoother input response.
*   **Performance Mode:** Enable this to make effects semi-transparent, allowing you to track projectiles and enemy positions more clearly.
*   **Ultimate Animations:** Disable these to prevent gameplay stutters and grind through stages significantly faster.

## Character Progression and AFK Management
As you progress past Chapter 2, you will unlock the ability to manage a roster of three characters, allowing you to swap between them mid-battle. This is crucial for handling specific boss mechanics, such as the Golem, where switching to a ranged mage allows you to maintain damage output while avoiding melee-range area-of-effect attacks.

### Managing Your AFK Journeys
The "Search" area is the heartbeat of your long-term progression. By deploying robots on 12-hour AFK journeys, you can passively farm essential resources.

| Resource Type | Acquisition Method | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **Memories** | AFK Search / Dungeons | High |
| **Gold** | AFK Search / Story | Medium |
| **Upgrade Materials** | Training Area | High |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always prioritize your AFK robot upgrades. These provide the consistent flow of materials needed to rank up your equipment and memories without needing to be actively engaged in combat 24/7.

## Mastering Class Customization
Once you reach Adventure Rank 8, you gain access to the skill tree. This is where you can truly specialize your characters. Rather than focusing on a single stat, look for passive abilities that complement your specific weapon loadout. If you are running a swordsman with a fire-based weapon, prioritize passives that increase fire damage or crit chance to maximize your synergy.
