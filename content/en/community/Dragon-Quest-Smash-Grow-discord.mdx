### S-Tier: The Gold Standard

The **Fire Blade** currently sits at the top of the hierarchy. While its base physical fire damage is a solid 250%, its true power lies in its ultimate ability. When an enemy is defeated, it launches two powerful fire projectiles. This creates a snowball effect during mob encounters and provides some of the highest total damage output in the game.

The **Falcon Blade** is the primary alternative for those who prefer speed. Dealing 350% potency physical wind damage, it offers the highest base hit in the game. Its ultimate grants immunity to movement and attack speed debuffs while conjuring wind spheres that provide excellent synergy with wind-type blessings.

### A-Tier: Ranged Excellence

For players who prefer to keep their distance, the **Shiver Stick** is the premier choice. It deals 225% magical ice damage and has a high chance to freeze enemies. Since freezing works on almost every enemy type, it provides unparalleled safety. The only drawback is its ultimate, which requires the player to take a hit to trigger a counter-attack—a counter-intuitive mechanic for a ranged character.

The **Queen's Whip** is the best option for pure light-elemental magic damage. It boosts attack speed for 10 seconds, making it a "glass cannon" favorite in the **Dragon Quest Smash Grow discord** community.

## Deep Dive: Why Some Weapons Fail

It is just as important to know what *not* to pull for. Weapons like the **Platinum Plumes** and **Crystal Claws** are often considered "trap" pulls. The Platinum Plumes rely on a silence mechanic (Spell Lock) which is irrelevant against the vast majority of physical-attacking enemies. Similarly, the Crystal Claws feature a "flinch" mechanic that is far too inconsistent to rely on during difficult boss encounters.

The **Sandstorm Spear** suffers from being too restrictive. To maximize its ultimate, you must possess specific "spike-type" blessings. Relying on RNG to get both the right weapon and the right blessings during a run makes this weapon far less reliable than the Fire Blade or Falcon Blade.

## Min-Maxing: The Metal Wing Boomerang

If you are a hardcore player looking to maximize your efficiency in 2026, you might consider the "Metal" banner. The **Metal Wing Boomerang** is a specialized tool that provides a +5% damage bonus against slimes. While this may seem small, it is essential for farming Liquid Metal Slimes and King Metal Slimes for massive experience gains later in the game.

However, keep in mind that this weapon is not a primary combat tool for bosses. Most members of the **Dragon Quest Smash Grow discord** recommend securing a top-tier sword first, then using secondary gems to hunt for the Metal Wing Boomerang as a utility swap.
