### Essential Settings for Optimal Gameplay

The game features a complex UI and intense combat scenarios, which can lead to visual clutter. Adjusting your settings immediately can drastically improve your performance, especially as you reach more difficult stages.

1. **Performance Mode:** Enable this in the system settings to make projectile effects semi-transparent. This is vital for tracking your character during chaotic boss fights.
2. **Frame Rate:** Set your game to 60 FPS to ensure smooth movement and responsive controls.
3. **Animations:** Turn off "Ultimate Animations" in the settings menu to save time during repetitive grinding sessions.
4. **Vibration:** Toggle this off if you prefer a cleaner, battery-efficient experience.

### Combat Mechanics and Character Swapping

The gameplay loop revolves around rogue-like survival, where you must navigate through waves of enemies. Mastering the ability to swap between your unlocked characters—typically a swordsman and a mage—is the key to surviving challenging encounters like the Golem gauntlet.

*   **Ranged Advantage:** Use your mage when the boss is performing area-of-effect attacks to maintain damage output from a safe distance.
*   **Melee Pressure:** Switch to your swordsman when you have an opening to maximize your burst damage.
*   **Auto-Play:** The game's AI is surprisingly adept at dodging projectiles. For standard content, utilizing the auto-play feature allows you to focus on character switching and timing your "Coup de Grace" ultimate abilities.
