## Mastering the Infinite Reroll System

Dragon Quest Smash Grow is remarkably generous with its starting summons. The game provides an infinite reroll banner that guarantees at least one three-star weapon. This is the foundation of your **dqsg tier** strategy. To access this, simply progress through the tutorial until the summon screen appears.

1.  **Initiate the Summon:** Tap the screen to trigger the animation.
2.  **Check the Color:** Look for the rainbow glow, which indicates a high-rarity item.
3.  **Evaluate the Result:** If you receive the Platinum Plums, lock them in immediately.
4.  **Reroll if Necessary:** If you receive a lower-tier weapon like the Sandstorm Spear or the Claw, simply hit the reroll button and try again. There is no limit to how many times you can do this.

## Character Classes and Team Synergy

While weapons are vital, the characters wielding them are equally important. You begin with a Swordsman, but as you progress through Chapter 2, you will unlock the Mage and eventually a third character slot. DQSG allows you to swap between these characters instantly during combat, which is a core mechanic for surviving boss encounters.

| Class | Combat Role | Best Paired With | Unlock Requirement |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Swordsman** | Melee DPS / Tank | Platinum Plums / Fire Blade | Default |
| **Mage** | Ranged AoE / CC | Magic Staffs | Clear Chapter 1 |
| **Third Slot** | Support / Utility | Various | Clear Chapter 2-2 |

When facing bosses like the Golem, who frequently uses a point-blank area attack, the best strategy is to switch to your Mage. This allows you to maintain consistent damage from a distance while the boss's melee-range hazards are active. Once the boss enters a vulnerable state, you can switch back to your Swordsman to unleash a high-damage "Coup de Grace" ultimate like the Inferno Slash.

## Performance Optimization for High-Level Play

As you climb the **dqsg tier** rankings and enter more difficult stages, the number of enemies and projectiles on the screen will increase exponentially. To maintain a competitive edge, you must optimize your game settings. The UI in DQSG can be cluttered, and visual effects can often obscure incoming threats.

### Essential Settings Checklist
*   **60 FPS Mode:** Found in the System menu. This is mandatory for reacting to fast-moving projectiles.
*   **Performance Mode:** This makes certain visual effects semi-transparent. In "bullet-hell" scenarios, this allows you to see your character's hitbox much more clearly.
*   **Ultimate Animations:** Turn these **OFF**. While the "Coup de Grace" cinematic is cool the first few times, it pauses the flow of combat and can be jarring during intense grinds.
*   **Vibrations:** Disable these to save battery life and reduce physical distractions during long play sessions.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to play DQSG on an emulator or certain tablets at launch. Many players have reported "stuck on loading" bugs. It is currently best optimized for modern smartphones.

## Progression Systems: Memories and the Tree

Beyond your initial weapon, your power in the **dqsg tier** hierarchy is determined by the "Memories" system and the "Tree" customization. Memories are small creatures you equip to your characters to gain passive stat boosts.

### The Memory System
Memories function similarly to equipment sets in other RPGs. By placing them in specific slots, you can unlock resonance bonuses. For example, stacking offensive-type Memories on your Swordsman will significantly boost his critical hit rate.

### The Class Tree
Once you reach **Adventure Rank 8**, you unlock the Tree. This is where the true customization begins. The Tree allows you to:
*   Unlock passive abilities for specific classes.
*   Increase the effectiveness of your Coup de Grace.
*   Enhance resource gain from AFK journeys.

## AFK Rewards and Resource Management

DQSG incorporates an "AFK Search" mechanic that is vital for long-term progression. You can send small robots on journeys that last up to 12 hours. These robots collect gold, upgrade materials, and even rare Memories while you are away from the game.

To maximize your efficiency:
1.  **Prioritize Robot Upgrades:** Spend your early gold on upgrading your search robots to increase the quality of loot they return.
2.  **Consistent Dispatch:** Always ensure your robots are on a journey before you log off for the night.
3.  **Gold Management:** Do not waste gold on upgrading C-tier weapons. Save your resources for S-tier gear like the Platinum Plums.

## Combat Tactics: The Golem Gauntlet

The Gauntlet is where you test your build against waves of enemies under a time limit. Unlike the story mode, the Gauntlet is focused entirely on survival and high scores.

*   **Auto-Play Utility:** The AI in DQSG is surprisingly competent. It will automatically dodge and kite enemies based on the character's range. If you find yourself overwhelmed, toggling Auto-Play can sometimes help you navigate out of a tight corner.
*   **Healing Boxes:** Keep an eye out for medicine boxes on the battlefield. In the Gauntlet, your HP does not regenerate between waves, making these pickups your only lifeline.
*   **Manual Ultimates:** While Auto-Play is good for movement, you should consider triggering your ultimates manually to ensure they hit the maximum number of enemies or are saved for elite spawns.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important weapon in the dqsg tier list for a new player?**

A: The **Platinum Plums** (Boomerang) is the most important weapon to aim for. Its returning projectile mechanics provide superior AoE damage, making it much easier to clear early-game mobs compared to melee weapons.

**Q: How do I unlock the third character slot in Dragon Quest Smash Grow?**

A: You must progress through the main story and complete **Stage 2-2**. Once cleared, the third slot will open, allowing you to bring a full three-person team into battle.

**Q: Should I turn on Performance Mode?**

A: Yes, absolutely. Performance Mode makes projectiles and effects semi-transparent, which is crucial for seeing your character during high-density combat phases where the screen is filled with enemies.

**Q: Is the infinite reroll truly infinite?**

A: Yes. You can continue to hit the reroll button on the starting three-star banner as many times as you like until you get the specific weapon you want. There is no penalty for rerolling multiple times.
