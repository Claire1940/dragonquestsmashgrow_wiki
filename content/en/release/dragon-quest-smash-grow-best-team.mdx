## Optimizing Performance for High-Level Combat

As you progress into more difficult stages, the screen will become cluttered with projectiles, enemies, and special effects. Even the **dragon quest smash grow best team** will fail if you cannot see incoming attacks. To prevent this, you must adjust your system settings for maximum clarity.

Go to the settings icon in the top right corner and navigate to the "System" and "Combat" menus. We recommend the following configuration for 2026:

*   **Performance Mode:** Turn this **ON**. It makes certain effects semi-transparent, allowing you to see your character's hitbox through the chaos of battle.
*   **Ultimate Animation:** Turn this **OFF**. While the "Coup de Grace" animations are beautiful, they interrupt the flow of combat and slow down your farming efficiency.
*   **Frame Rate:** Set this to **60 FPS** to ensure smooth dodging and character swapping.
*   **Vibration:** Turn this **OFF** to save battery life during long AFK sessions.

## Enhancing Your Team with Memories and the Tree

Beyond weapons and levels, two advanced systems dictate the strength of your squad: **Memories** and the **Tree**.

### The Memory System
Memories are collectible cards featuring classic Dragon Quest creatures. These are equipped to your characters to provide passive stat boosts. The game features a unique "Order" system where placing Memories in specific sequences can trigger bonus set effects. Always check the tutorial prompts in the Forge area to ensure you are maximizing these synergies.

### The Skill Tree
Upon reaching **Adventure Rank 8**, you unlock the Tree. This is where the true customization begins. The Tree allows you to:
*   Customize class-specific passives.
*   Unlock new active abilities for your characters.
*   Boost the overall stats of your entire team.

| **Search Area** | Chapter 2 | AFK resource generation |

## Resource Management: The Search Area

Maintaining the **dragon quest smash grow best team** requires a constant influx of gold, upgrade materials, and Memories. The "Search" area is your primary source of passive income. In this mode, you send small robots on journeys that last up to 12 hours.

It is vital to prioritize upgrading your Search robots. Higher-level robots return with better loot, including rare Memories and premium currency. Always keep your robots deployed, even when you are not actively playing the game, to ensure you have the resources needed to forge new equipment as soon as you hit a progression wall.
