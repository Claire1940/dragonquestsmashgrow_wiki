## SS-Tier: The King of Metal Hunting

At the absolute peak of the **smash grow tier list** sits the **Metal Wing Boomerang**. This weapon is widely considered the "must-have" pull for any serious player in 2026. Its dominance stems from two specific factors: its effectiveness in Metal Quests and its ricochet enchantment.

The Metal Wing Boomerang deals significantly increased damage to the metal family, including Metal Slimes, Metal Medleys, and Liquid Metal Slimes. Since these enemies are the primary source of experience points, having a weapon that can efficiently dispatch them is invaluable. Furthermore, its enchantment allows normal ranged attacks to ricochet once upon impact, effectively doubling your damage output when facing groups of enemies.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Use the Metal Wing Boomerang's Ultimate (Coup de Grace) specifically when Liquid Metal Slimes appear to ensure you don't miss out on massive XP gains before they flee.

## S and A-Tier: High-Performance Melee

While the boomerang reigns supreme for farming, the **Metal Slime Sword** and **Fire Blade** are the workhorses of the campaign. The Metal Slime Sword offers a massive boost to attack speed for five seconds after a final normal attack, allowing for rapid-fire strikes that shred through enemy health bars.

The **Fire Blade** is a unique entry in the **smash grow tier list** because of its defensive utility. While its 250% physical fire damage is impressive, its true value lies in the 15-second immunity to freeze. As you progress into the deeper, ice-themed levels of the 2026 campaign, being frozen can lead to an instant "Game Over." This weapon negates that risk entirely while providing powerful fire projectiles upon defeating enemies.

## Utility and Ranged Options

The **Platinum Plums** (another boomerang variant) and the **Crystal Claws** offer utility that raw damage weapons lack. The Platinum Plums are particularly notable for their ability to inflict **Spell Lock**. In early stages, you might not notice the impact, but in the 2026 endgame content, enemy casters can devastate your team with high-level magic. Locking their spells is often the only way to survive certain encounters.

The **Crystal Claws** act as a hybrid between melee and range. They allow the player to launch lightning-infused arrows that pierce through multiple enemies. This is an excellent alternative for players who haven't yet pulled a top-tier metal weapon but still need to clear waves of enemies quickly.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Ranged weapons like the **Shiver Stick** generally have lower base damage than their melee counterparts. Only use them in stages where terrain (like water gaps) prevents you from reaching enemies.

## Lower Tier Weapons: Why They Struggle

Not every weapon in the **smash grow tier list** can be a winner. Weapons like the **Queen's Whip** and **Sandstorm Spear** suffer from being too niche. The Queen's Whip deals light damage and provides an attack speed ward, but the damage numbers are significantly lower than the swords or boomerangs.

The **Sandstorm Spear** is currently the weakest weapon in the meta. It relies heavily on "Spike Type Blessings" to trigger its earth-infused attacks. If you do not get the specific blessings during your run, the weapon's effectiveness drops significantly. Unless you are facing an enemy with a specific 2x weakness to Earth, you are better off using a neutral S-tier weapon.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon in the smash grow tier list?**

A: The **Metal Wing Boomerang** is currently considered the best weapon due to its ricochet ability and its specialized damage against metal slime enemies, which are vital for leveling up.

**Q: Are ranged weapons better than melee weapons in Smash Grow?**

A: Generally, no. Melee weapons like the Metal Slime Sword and Falcon Blade tend to have much higher damage percentages. Ranged weapons are mostly used for specific training quests where enemies are separated by water or platforms.

**Q: How do I unlock the enchantments on my weapons?**

A: Enchantments are typically acquired at the start of a quest. Some require specific conditions to be met, such as finishing a normal attack combo or possessing a certain number of "Blessings" during a run.

**Q: Should I pull on the featured Metal Banner?**

A: Yes. The featured banner containing the Metal Wing Boomerang and Metal Slime Sword is the most efficient use of your gems in 2026, as these weapons provide the most utility for both farming and progression.
