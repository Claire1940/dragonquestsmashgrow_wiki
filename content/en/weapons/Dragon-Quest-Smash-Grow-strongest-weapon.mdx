## High-Performance Melee Options (A+ to A Tier)

If you haven't been lucky enough to pull a Metal-series item from the featured banners, several other options serve as the **Dragon Quest Smash Grow strongest weapon** for general progression.

### Fire Blade
The Fire Blade is a sleeper hit in the current 2026 meta. While its 250% physical fire damage is respectable, its true value lies in the **15-second immunity to Freeze**. In the deeper levels of the game, environmental hazards and enemy spells can lock your character in place, leading to a quick defeat. The Fire Blade negates this mechanic entirely.

### Falcon Blade
For pure DPS enthusiasts, the Falcon Blade offers a devastating 350% physical wind damage ultimate. This is one of the highest percentage modifiers in the game. After using the ultimate, you gain two wind-infused spheres that orbit your character, providing passive damage and protection for 10 seconds.

### Crystal Claws
Crystal Claws are widely considered the best non-metal alternative for slime hunting. The "Flashing Talons" transformation causes enemies to flinch, which is vital for preventing Metal Slimes from fleeing the battle. Its enchantment also launches a piercing lightning arrow that can clear entire rows of enemies.

## How to Optimize Your Weapon Loadout

To truly harness the power of the **Dragon Quest Smash Grow strongest weapon**, you must pair it with the correct blessings and enchantments. The game rewards synergy over raw stats.

1.  **Check Elemental Weaknesses**: Before entering a dungeon, check the enemy preview. Using a Fire Blade against fire-resistant enemies will significantly hamper your clear time.
2.  **Synergize with Blessings**: The Shiver Stick, for example, sees its area of attack expand drastically if you possess four "Great Arrow" type blessings. Without these, it remains a mid-tier weapon at best.
3.  **Manage Your Enchantments**: Some enchantments trigger on "Quest Start," while others trigger on "Enemy Defeated." For boss-heavy stages, "Quest Start" enchantments like the Metal Slime Sword's speed boost are far more reliable.

**Q: Is the Shiver Stick worth upgrading for the late game?**
